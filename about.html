About Us -

Satdharal Adventures is a dynamic Travel Agency managed by adventure tourism professionals from the industry. We are approved by Department of tourism - Government of India Specialises in Package Tours, Trekking and Jeep safari in Kullu - Manali , Shimla, Kinnaur, Lahaul and Spiti, Leh - Ladakh. Kangra, Chamba, Rewalsar.. From short treks and to the chauffeur driven jeep safaris to the remotes of the India Himalaya. We also organize high altitude treks such as the Thamsar Pass,Jalsu pass Kugti Pass Dharcha - Lamayuru and jeep safaris to Ladakh and Spiti and Kinnaur, as well as sporting event.

Our Mission -

To provide a highly innovative, quality and personalised service to ensure a memorable and trouble free holiday experience.

Our Equipments -

Jeeps / Accommodations best quality 3 / 4 season high altitude Dom tents. Warm down feather double cover sleeping bags. Carry mats. Harnesses, Ropes, Ice Axes and carabineers. Scorpios and Toyota Qualis jeeps which are in good condition and not older than 3 years are only employed for your travels. The very best Hotels / Guest Houses / Cottages / Camps are used at each destination
